Window Wells

BILDEX Window Well Systems are the solution for your leaky basement windows. This excellent state of the art product has a compact design to direct light into your basement space.
This product has a fiberglass construction, operable gate, bottom drain, adjustable height and direct weeping tile connection. Additionally, BILDEX Window Wells are galvanized and durable, making this product easy to install.
BILDEX Window Well Systems have zero maintenance and come with a 5-year warranty.

Installations

It's Easy! BILDEX Window Well Systems are easy to install. They connect directly to the existing weeping tile. All products come with weeping tile adapter, which is typical for all models.
