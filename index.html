Window Wells

PRODUCT ADVANTAGES AT A GLANCE
Adjustable in heights With its integrated height-adjustable attachment, the Buildex window well can be adjusted by up to 25 cm to align it with ground level, either during installation or subsequently - without having to drill new holes, purchase any additional accessories or waste valuable time
4 sizes One light well in four different sizes with the right accessories for every situation: car passable with suitable gratings
Thermal bridge-free mounting Thermal bridge-free or thermal bridge-free and water pressure-tight installation when used with the Buildex insulated mounting panel and a suitable fastening set

Features

BILDEX Window Well Systems are the solution for your leaky basement windows. This excellent state of the art product has a compact design to direct light into your basement space.
This product has a fiberglass construction, operable gate, bottom drain, adjustable height and direct weeping tile connection. Additionally, BILDEX Window Wells are galvanized and durable, making this product easy to install.
BILDEX Window Well Systems have zero maintenance and come with a 5-year warranty.

Installations

It's Easy! BILDEX Window Well Systems are easy to install. They connect directly to the existing weeping tile. All products come with weeping tile adapter, which is typical for all models.
